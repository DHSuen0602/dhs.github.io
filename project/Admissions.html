Admissions Overview

General Entry Requirements

  • SPM/O-Level: Minimum 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • STPM/A-Level: Minimum 2 Principal Passes
  • Diploma: Minimum CGPA of 2.00 for undergraduate entry
  • UEC: Minimum 5 credits including English and Mathematics

How to Apply

  • Step 1: Choose your desired program
  • Step 2: Prepare supporting documents (IC, certificates, transcripts)
  • Step 3: Complete the online application form
  • Step 4: Wait for confirmation and offer letter

Application Deadlines

July 2025 Intake: Application closes on June 30, 2025
November 2025 Intake: Application closes on October 15, 2025

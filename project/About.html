About Us

Established in 2020, the University of FutureTech is a leading institution in Malaysia dedicated to shaping the next generation of innovators, thinkers, and global citizens. We combine technology, creativity, and research to transform education and empower students to change the world.

Our Vision

To be a world-class university that leads in future-ready education, innovation, and digital transformation.

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality education with global relevance
  • Foster innovation through interdisciplinary learning
  • Support student success through inclusivity and support
  • Engage with industry and communities for real-world impact

Why Choose FutureTech?

  • Modern campus with smart classrooms and innovation labs
  • Industry partnerships with top tech firms and startups
  • Student-centered learning environment
  • Strong international exchange programs
